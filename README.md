# Walls & Balls

You have a ball and that's it! What more could you want?

## Game Story

Inside four walls, there is a ball. And yes, this ball has a chance… maybe.

The arena looks simple, but it is not. Each wall has surprises, and some very helpful holes, so staying inside is harder than it sounds.

Your goal is clear: keep the ball inside the borders.

You choose the direction. The ball moves, hits the walls, and bounces. Every hit matters.

## How It Works

Each level gives you a number.

This number shows how many touches you need.

Every time the ball hits a wall, the number goes down by 1.

Simple, right?

Bring the number to **zero** while staying inside the walls… and you win.

Different levels, different numbers. Sometimes easy. Sometimes not.

## The Rule (Yes, Only One)

Finish all required touches **without leaving the walls**.

If the ball escapes through a hole… well…

You become food. Like real life.

## Final Words

Aim carefully. Angles matter.

Or just send it and hope for the best.

Sometimes skill helps.

Sometimes luck does all the work.
